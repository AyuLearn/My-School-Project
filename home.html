COVID-19

COVID-19 is a virus that can spread by person-to-person or community-to-community. This virus is spread from Wuhan, China and it causes many deaths and many are suffering from it. The virus that causes COVID-19 is in a family of viruses called Coronaviridae. Some people who become ill from this virus can also develop a bacterial infection as a complication. Goverment spread awareness to people that to maintain social distancing and sanitize their hands. In India, This virus has a major impact.

The impact of COVID-19 in India (In 10 December 2020) :

  1. Total Cases : 9.74M+
  2. Total Recoverd : 9.22M+
  3. Total Deaths : 141K+
